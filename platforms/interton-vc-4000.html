Interton VC 4000

Released: 1978

37 games

The VC 4000 (short for Video Computer 4000) is an early second generation 8-bit cartridge-based home video game console released in Germany in 1978 by Interton. The console is quite obscure outside Germany, but many software-compatible systems can be found in many European countries.
